# Executive Intelligence Briefing (EIB)

## Our Guiding Principle

> Every solution should leave behind a better starting point for the next person.

The Executive Intelligence Briefing exists to capture knowledge, organize experience, and preserve solutions in a way that others can build upon.

Sometimes the "next person" is a coworker.

Sometimes it's your successor.

Sometimes it's your family.

Quite often, it's your future self.

Our goal is not merely to solve problems.

Our goal is to leave behind a better starting point.

---

## Public vs Private

Every artifact created within the EIB should be evaluated using two simple questions:

1. Can this help someone else?
2. Does it contain personal information?

If it can help others, it belongs in the Public repository.

If it contains personal information, it belongs in the Private repository.

If it belongs in both, create a reusable template in Public and personalize it in Private.

---

## Continuous Improvement

Every document should become better over time.

Every solution should become easier to reuse.

Every lesson learned should reduce future effort.

That is the purpose of the Executive Intelligence Briefing.
